About QuickBrief

QuickBrief is a comprehensive toolkit for virtual pilots, providing essential tools and calculators to enhance your flight simulation experience. From extracting key information from SimBrief briefings to calculating optimal descent points, QuickBrief helps you plan and execute your virtual flights with precision.

Features

  • SimBrief Briefing Extractor
  • Real-time METAR and Weather Information
  • Top of Descent Calculator
  • Wind Correction Calculator
  • Fuel Planning Calculator
  • Multi-language Support
